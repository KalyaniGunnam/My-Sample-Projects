Hello, I'm Kalyani

I am an offbeat traveller and keep a tryst of finding beautiful places to visit and eating outlets to satisfy my taste buds. This blog is to introduce my taste for travel and food! One of the best things I love about travelling is discovering unique food that each country offers. I believe food is reflection of culture and traditional values of that particular place. The types of spices, the way it is cooked are all dependent on the rich heritage that these countries are pursuing.

One of the other factors which keeps me going and helps me in chasing my dream of travelling each and every part of the globe is photography. Taking and preserving pictures not only takes me back to my days of travelling but also provides a sudden escape to this monotonous life.

Traveling and finding yourself is good for the soul. Here are my major takeaways:

  • There are always new passions to discover.
  • Life does not have to progress in a linear manner.
  • It doesn’t cost anything to be nice to strangers.
  • There is nothing to fear, but fear itself.
  • Sometimes, we say more by listening.
  • Serendipity is not scarce.
  • Most beliefs are mandated by circumstance.
  • Relationships should be easy.
  • Every day is a chance to learn.
